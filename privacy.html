Privacy Policy for AI Article Detector

Last updated: 9 March, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the AI Article Detector Chrome extension handles information when you use it. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Information Collection and Use

The AI Article Detector extension does not collect any personal information from users. The extension only processes article content from websites you choose to analyze. This content is:

  • Analyzed for AI-generated content detection
  • Temporarily stored for pattern analysis purposes
  • Not linked to your personal identity in any way

Article Data Storage

When you use the extension to analyze an article:

  • The article URL and text content are sent to our analysis service
  • The domain name is extracted for pattern analysis
  • Analysis results are stored to improve detection accuracy

No personally identifying information is collected or stored during this process.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any information to external parties.

Third-Party Services

Our extension does not include or integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
